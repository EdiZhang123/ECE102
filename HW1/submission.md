## EE102 HW 1 Submission Instructions

There are two components: written answers and coding answers.

Please write your written answers on a separate piece of paper/on an iPad/similar and then scan as a PDF and upload to Gradescope.

For the coding answers, please *run all cells* on the notebook, then download the notebook as a PDF and submit that long with your written answers. Make sure the results of your code are visible on the PDF so we can grade them.

Please submit both PDFs to the same assignment and tag questions appropriately.
